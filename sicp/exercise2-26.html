Exercise 2.26. Suppose we define x and y to be two lists:

(define x (list 1 2 3))
(define y (list 4 5 6))

What result is printed by the interpreter in response to evaluating each of the following expressions:

(append x y)

(cons x y)

(list x y)

将练习依次输入解释器

1 ]=> (define x (list 1 2 3))

;Value: x

1 ]=> (define y (list 4 5 6))

;Value: y

1 ]=> (append x y)

;Value 5: (1 2 3 4 5 6)

1 ]=> (cons x y)

;Value 6: ((1 2 3) 4 5 6)

1 ]=> (list x y)

;Value 7: ((1 2 3) (4 5 6))
